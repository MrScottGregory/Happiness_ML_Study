Data & Presentation

In a week's time, we were able to accomplish data cleaning, aggregation and reshaping the indicators for modeling using data from three data sources.Each analyst was given spreadsheets with data per year as well as linear data for regarding the data indicators.

However, in respect to their model, that data was consolidated or refined further to properly handle the limits of their models.

While each of them had the same data source, the results vary slightly depending on the data each model had available.

To present this data we decided that webpage would meet our long term aspirations for our work & Each page was written in the voice of the analyst and was modified to best represent their model. .

Conclusion

Though our models did not return the exact same results, there was some notable overlap. Three of the four models identified "Average Wages" as one of the most important features. "GDP","Population Density", "Healthy Life Expectancy", and "Democratic Quality" also surfaced across multiple models. Ultimately, no one feature alone could predict Happiness precisely. It was only when we aggregated three or four top features that we could make predictions with any confidence.
